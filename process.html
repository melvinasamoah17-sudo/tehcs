Our Process

Four clear steps from your first contact to a completed, reviewed job — transparent, professional and stress-free from start to finish.

How It Works

Simple from
start to finish.

We've designed every step to be clear and hassle-free so you can focus on what matters — your family and your home.

01
Step One

Assessment &
Requirement Gathering

We start by listening. Our team meets with you and your family — in person or remotely — to understand your specific needs, living situation, preferences, and any health or safety considerations. This consultation is completely free and comes with no obligations.

02
Step Two

Planning &
Resource Allocation

Based on your needs, we create a detailed service plan and match the right professionals to your case. You receive a transparent proposal with clear timelines and pricing — no hidden fees, no surprises. We only proceed once you're fully satisfied.

03
Step Three

Service Execution
& Delivery

Our qualified team arrives on time, fully equipped and ready to deliver. Whether it's a home nurse, construction crew or housekeeper — they work with professionalism, care and respect for your space and family throughout the entire engagement.

04
Step Four

Monitoring, Evaluation
& Closure

Once complete, we conduct a quality review and gather your feedback. For ongoing care, we continuously monitor and adapt the plan as your needs change. Your satisfaction — and ongoing safety — never expires with TEHCS.

FAQs

Common questions answered.

How quickly can TEHCS start a service?

In most cases we can begin within 24–72 hours of your initial consultation. For emergency care needs we work hard to provide same-day or next-day support where possible.

Are all TEHCS staff background-checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every team member — nurse, tradesperson or domestic worker — undergoes thorough background checks, reference verification and credential validation before joining TEHCS.

Can I request the same carer or worker each visit?

Yes. Consistency matters to us too. We do our best to assign the same person to your household and will only change staff if you request it or in exceptional circumstances.

Do you operate in both Ghana and the UK?

Yes. TEHCS operates in Ghana (Accra and surrounding regions) and the United Kingdom. Contact numbers for both countries are listed on our Contact page.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Contact us immediately and we will resolve the issue at no additional cost — including reassignment of staff if needed.
