Our Story

Built on the belief that every home deserves a shine.

TEHCS — Twitch Empire Home Care Service — was founded with a single conviction: that families everywhere deserve access to reliable, professional home care and home services delivered with genuine heart.

We provide care services to elderly, disabled, and people of all ages and physical conditions who would like to remain living at home, yet require assistance with certain daily, weekly and monthly activities.

Working closely with clients and their families we provide professional personalized assistance in client's own home, hospital, long-term assisted living facilities and other places — across Ghana and the United Kingdom.
